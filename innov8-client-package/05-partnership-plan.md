## The Opportunity

As Innov8 Creations grows its client base, there will be demand for services beyond what Alejandro can deliver alone — website development, custom digital tools, social media creative packs, SEO. Rather than turning those clients away or building an in-house team (with the overhead that comes with it), Innov8 can outsource that work to us on a commission-based model.

**You bring the client. We do the work. Everyone wins.**

---

## How It Works

### The Model
Innov8 Creations acts as the client-facing agency. We act as the invisible production studio behind the scenes. Alejandro presents the work as Innov8's own offering — we handle execution.

This is standard practice in the agency world. It's called a **white-label partnership**.

### Commission Structure

| Service Category | Innov8 Marks Up | We Charge Innov8 | Innov8's Margin |
|-----------------|-----------------|------------------|-----------------|
| Website (Landing Page) | $1,200–$2,000 to client | $700–$1,000 to us | $500–$1,000 |
| Website (Business, 4–6 pages) | $3,500–$6,000 to client | $2,000–$3,500 to us | $1,500–$2,500 |
| Monthly SEO Retainer | $600–$1,500/mo to client | $300–$800/mo to us | $300–$700/mo |
| Social Media Creative Pack | $500–$1,000/mo to client | $250–$600/mo to us | $250–$400/mo |
| Email Campaign Setup | $1,000–$2,000 to client | $600–$1,000 to us | $400–$1,000 |

> Pricing is flexible and can be adjusted per project based on scope and complexity. We always provide a detailed quote before any work begins.

## Getting Started

When Innov8 has a client ready for a website or any of the above services, the process is simple:

1. Alejandro sends us the client brief (we provide a template)
2. We return a quote within 48 hours
3. Alejandro marks it up and presents to the client
4. Client approves → 50% deposit paid → we begin
5. We deliver → Alejandro reviews → client receives the work

No meetings required for routine projects. Clean, efficient, professional.
