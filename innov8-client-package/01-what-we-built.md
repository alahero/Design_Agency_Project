## 1. Brand Identity & Guidelines

We established Innov8 Creations as a premium, nationally-positioned design and marketing agency. Every brand decision was made with intention, anchored to a clear visual and verbal identity system.

### Visual Identity
- **Color System** — A three-color palette engineered for trust, energy, and technology:
  - **Navy Blue `#0A1F44`** — Institutional authority and stability
  - **Red `#FF2D2D`** — American energy, urgency, and boldness
  - **Cyan `#00B4FF`** — Innovation, technology, and forward momentum
- **Typography** — Montserrat (headings) paired with Inter (body), selected for their modern professionalism and Google-optimized performance
- **Logo Suite** — Multiple vector logo versions created including:
  - Full horizontal wordmark
  - Reduced / icon-only version
  - Dark and light variants
  - Favicon-optimized SVG (cyan fill)
- **Brand Mood** — *National Reach, Local Dedication. Result-Driven. Modern. Premium.*

### Tone of Voice
Defined as **The Expert Patriot** — a voice that is:
- **Professional** — industry-standard terminology, always accessible
- **Proud** — confident about national reach across all 50 states
- **Action-Oriented** — focused on Leads, Growth, and Results
- Vocabulary: "engineer," "high-performance," "result-driven," "strategic," "measurable"

### Brand Strategy Foundation
- Core Identity: *"National Reach, Local Dedication"*
- Positioning: Premium design partner for US-market businesses that need results, not just aesthetics
- Target Client: Small-to-medium businesses looking to scale with a professional brand presence

## 2. Website Design & Development

A fully custom PHP website built from the ground up with a cinematic, premium aesthetic.

### Pages Delivered
| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `index.php` | Homepage — Hero, Portfolio Preview, Services, Process, Packages |
| `portfolio.php` | Full bento-grid portfolio with 6 real project modals and dynamic filtering |
| `contact.php` | Lead generation contact form with service selector |
| `404.php` | Custom branded error page |

### Technical Stack
- **PHP** — Server-side rendering, modular includes structure
- **Tailwind CSS 4** — Utility-first design system with custom brand tokens
- **GSAP + ScrollTrigger** — Cinematic scroll animations and entrance effects
- **Lucide Icons** — Consistent SVG icon system
- **Google Fonts** — Montserrat + Inter (preloaded for performance)

### SEO & Technical Implementation
- Unique `<title>` and `<meta description>` per page (within character limits)
- Open Graph + Twitter Card tags on all pages
- Schema.org `ProfessionalService` JSON-LD structured data
- Canonical URLs
- Favicon (SVG, light/dark compatible)
- OG Image (1200×630px) generated for social sharing
- `loading="lazy"` on all below-fold images
- `rel="noopener noreferrer"` on all external links
- Dynamic copyright year (`date('Y')`)
- Custom 404 page

### Portfolio Integration
- 6 real projects photographed, formatted, and integrated
- Projects organized across 3 service categories: Logo & Branding, Editorial Design, Social Media
- Interactive bento-grid modal for each project (cover + content media)
