### 3. A Market-Intelligence Competitor Analysis

We researched 61 of the most established design agencies in New York City and San Francisco — the two most competitive markets in the country.

**What we found:**
- 47% of competitors have a perfect 5-star rating — but most with under 10 reviews, meaning low actual volume
- Most agencies are hyper-local and aesthetics-focused — no national scope, no results-first language
- Virtually none have a visible, structured pricing system
- The best-performing agencies all use dark, cinematic, editorial design — exactly what Innov8 now uses

**What this means for you:**
Innov8 is now positioned *above* the average agency in these markets — in design quality, positioning, and strategic clarity — without being based in their expensive zip codes.
