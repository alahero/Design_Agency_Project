## Phase 1 — Immediate Actions (No Extra Budget Required)

These are high-leverage moves Alejandro can execute today using what he already has.

### 1. Launch the Website & Claim the Domain
- Deploy the website to a production PHP host (Hostinger, SiteGround, or Cloudways recommended — $5–$20/month)
- Secure `innov8creations.com` or your preferred domain immediately
- Connect Google Analytics and Google Search Console (free)

### 2. Google Business Profile
- Claim and fully complete the Innov8 Google Business Profile
- Add all 6 portfolio projects as photos
- Set service areas to all 50 states
- **Ask the first 5 clients for a Google Review this week** — this is the single highest-ROI action available at zero cost

### 3. Activate Social Media with Brand Assets
- Profile photos → use the Icon logo (cyan on dark)
- Cover images → use the horizontal wordmark
- Bio copy → *"National Reach. Local Dedication. Premium brand identity & digital design for businesses across all 50 states."*
- Priority platforms: Instagram (visual portfolio), LinkedIn (B2B leads)

### 4. Use the Portfolio Actively
- Share each of the 6 portfolio projects as a dedicated post on Instagram and LinkedIn
- Tag clients (with their permission) for amplification
- Use this structure: *Before → After → Result*

### 5. Activate the Contact Form
- Set up form submission notifications (email or Slack)
- Respond to every inquiry within 2 hours — speed is your first brand impression

## Phase 2 — Growth (3–6 Months)

### 1. Build Out the Portfolio to 12+ Projects
- Document every new client project with photos/video
- Aim for 2 projects per service category
- Prioritize diverse industries to demonstrate national scope

### 2. Create a Case Study Format
- Per project: Client challenge → Innov8's approach → Measurable result
- 3 case studies on the website = significant trust increase

### 3. Referral System
- Formalize a referral incentive: *"Refer a business that signs with us, earn $X credit toward future services"*
- Ask every delivered client within 30 days of completion
